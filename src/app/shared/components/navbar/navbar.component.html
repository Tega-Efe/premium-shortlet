About Sweet Homes

Our Story

Sweet Homes was founded with a simple vision: to revolutionize the short-term rental experience in Lagos. We understand that finding quality accommodation that feels like home can be challenging, especially when you're visiting a new city for business or pleasure.

Our premium two-bedroom apartment in Victoria Island represents our commitment to excellence. Every detail has been carefully curated to ensure you have a comfortable, luxurious, and memorable stay.

What We Offer

  • Prime Location: Located in the heart of Victoria Island, Lagos, with easy access to business districts, shopping centers, and entertainment venues.
  • Luxury Amenities: Fully furnished apartments with modern appliances, high-speed internet, air conditioning, and premium bedding.
  • Flexible Booking: Whether you need accommodation for a few days or several months, we offer flexible booking options to suit your needs.
  • 24/7 Support: Our dedicated team is available round the clock to ensure your stay is seamless and comfortable.
  • Safety & Security: We prioritize your safety with secure premises, CCTV surveillance, and verified booking processes.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional short-term rental experiences that combine the comfort of home with the luxury of a five-star hotel. We strive to be the trusted choice for business travelers, tourists, and anyone seeking quality accommodation in Lagos.
